# DataWiz UTM URL Builder

Every time you share a link in an ad, an email, or a social post, you want to know which of those links actually brought people to your website. That's what campaign tags (UTMs) are for. The problem is that they're easy to get wrong: one person writes "Facebook", another writes "fb", and suddenly your reports are split into pieces that don't add up.

The DataWiz URL Builder solves that. It's a simple web tool where your whole team creates campaign links the same way, every time.

## How it works

1. **Sign in with your Google account.** No new passwords to remember.
2. **Paste the page you want to link to** and pick your channel (email, paid social, Google Ads, and so on). The tool fills in recommended values for that channel and guides you through the rest.
3. **Get a friendly nudge if something looks off.** The tool spots capital letters, spaces, addresses that don't look like real web pages, and even different names for the same thing (like "fb" in one link and "facebook" in another). You stay in control, but mistakes get caught before they reach your reports.
4. **Click Generate, then Copy.** Grab the full link, or just the tag portion if you only need that, and paste it wherever your campaign goes live.

## Channels that match how you work

- Start with a ready-made list of common channels, each with sensible values already filled in.
- Reorder the list so your most-used channels sit at the top, and hide the ones you never use.
- Edit any channel's default values to fit your own naming conventions, or create your own custom channels from scratch.
- Changed your mind? Restore the default list with one click.

## Built for teams

- Invite colleagues with a simple link, so everyone builds URLs in one shared workspace.
- Values your team uses often (campaign names, sources) can be saved once and reused by everyone. Saved a typo by accident? Anyone on the team can remove it.
- Every link anyone creates is saved automatically to a shared history, organized by month, showing who made it and when.
- Keep the history tidy: filter it, copy past links, and delete test links or mistakes, one at a time or in bulk.
- Need the data elsewhere? Export your full link history to a spreadsheet in one click.

## And the practical details

- A built-in UTM tagging guide you can download and share with your team.
- Works just as well on your phone as on your desktop.
- Your account, your data: you can leave a team or delete your account at any time.

The result: clean, consistent campaign links, no spreadsheets full of copy-paste formulas, and reports you can actually trust.
